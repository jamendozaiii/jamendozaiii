# ⚙️ Data Engineering Portfolio

As a Data Analyst with a deep focus on backend processes, I specialize in building the infrastructure that makes clean, automated analytics possible. My approach combines robust ETL pipelines with Kaizen principles to eliminate manual bottlenecks and ensure data integrity.

## 🌟 Featured Architecture: Automated Campus Data Centralization

**Objective:** Architect an automated data pipeline to ingest, clean, and centralize decentralized campus data sources into a unified dimensional model for executive reporting.

**Tech Stack:** Power BI (Data Modeling & Power Query), SQL, Python

### Engineering Highlights & Business Impact:
*   **Data Ingestion & Centralization:** Engineered the ingestion process for disparate, siloed campus data sources, merging them into a single, reliable source of truth.
*   **Dimensional Modeling:** Designed a robust Star Schema architecture to ensure optimal query performance, logical data relationships, and scalability for future data sources.
*   **ETL Pipeline Automation:** Automated the extraction and transformation processes, replacing manual data preparation with streamlined workflows.
*   **Process Improvement (Kaizen):** Applied continuous improvement methodologies to reduce reporting latency, shifting from manual data aggregation to an automated, near real-time refresh cycle.
