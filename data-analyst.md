# 📊 Data Analytics Portfolio

My analytics philosophy is simple: data is only as good as the decisions it drives. I specialize in translating complex datasets into clear, actionable insights through intuitive dashboards and executive reporting, always looking for ways to continuously improve processes (Kaizen).

## 🌟 Featured Project: Executive Campus Dashboard

**Objective:** Translate centralized, complex campus data into an intuitive, high-level executive dashboard to drive strategic decision-making.

**Tech Stack:** Power BI, Tableau, Looker, SQL, Python

### Analytics Highlights & Business Impact:
*   **Executive Reporting & KPI Tracking:** Designed intuitive visualizations that distill massive datasets into clear, actionable KPIs for stakeholders and leadership.
*   **Stakeholder Visibility:** Bridged the gap between raw data and business strategy, providing leadership with real-time visibility into critical campus metrics.
*   **UI/UX & Interactive Design:** Applied best practices in dashboard design, utilizing advanced formatting, dynamic filtering, and slicer configurations to ensure a seamless user experience.
*   **Actionable Insights:** Replaced static, manual reports with dynamic dashboards, empowering decision-makers to identify trends and act quickly.
